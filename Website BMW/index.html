BMW M3 GTR Le Mans

The BMW M3 GTR (E46) didn't compete in the 24 Hours of Le Mans. but had a legendary history in the 2001 American Le Mans Series (ALMS).
It used a purpose-built P60B40 V8 engine for its single season.

BMW M4 DTM

BMW M4 DTM was a touring car that raced from 2014 to 2020,
replacing the BMW M3 DTM and featuring two distinct versions :
a naturally-aspirated V8 for the first few seasons and a turbo Class 1 model, starting in 2019.

BMW M4 GT3 EVO

BMW M4 GT3 Evo is the 2025 evolution of BMW M4 GT3,
debuted in 2022 after M4 GT3 was revealed in December 2020. The Evo model features improvements to aerodynamics, engine, chassis, and serviceability.
